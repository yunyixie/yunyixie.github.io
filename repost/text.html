Fuck You: A Magazine of the Arts (1962-1965) Fuck You: A Magazine of the Arts was an absolutely vital literary magazine
founded in 1962 by the poet Ed Sanders and published from a “secret location
on the Lower East Side” of New York City. Probably the most important mimeo
revolution publication. Sanders’ Peace Eye Bookstore was raided by police on
January 1, 1966 and Saunders was charged with obscenity in no small part due
to the contents of Fuck You. Notoriety generated by the case led to his
appearance on the February 17, 1967 cover of Life Magazine, which proclaimed
him "a leader of New York's Other Culture."
