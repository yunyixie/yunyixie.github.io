This is LEVEL 3 C Assume that all of the information and imagery was supplied by the culture, that none of the information or imagery is original. Research the subject as if you know nothing about it. And don’t stop until you have something interesting, or even better, something original to say. That’s the most likely way of producing an original image. The design process can begin only after you are satisfied with the statement.
